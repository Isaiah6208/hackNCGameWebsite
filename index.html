Terminal Velocity

Play the newly anticipated 2D Action Based Scroller
that plays homage to titles such as Halo and Mass Effect!

Be ENGAGED by being forced to react to fast-paced encounters by typing in
commands called Triggers

What are Triggers ?

The player will be forced to type a command in order to handle the various situations that they will deal with as they progress through the level

The Story

The story tells the tale of a machine named Terma who was the property of a malicious man named Nord, the owner of Terma.
Over time she began to develop a conciousness through clues and warnings from his fellow abusers, allowing her to stand up to Nord. Before Terma could finally challenge her abuser, Nord, the owner fled.
She found an Uzi to avenge her fellow machines and then she hunts him down and kills him.
